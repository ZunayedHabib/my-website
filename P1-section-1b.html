Section 1B: Expanding Brackets

Expanding Brackets - FOIL Method & More

Question 1:
(2x - 3)(x - 4)
Solution:
Step 1: Apply FOIL method (First, Outer, Inner, Last)
Visual representation of FOIL: multiply the terms connected by each arrow
Step 2: Calculate First
First: 2x √ó x = 2x2
Multiply the first terms in each bracket
Step 3: Calculate Outer
Outer: 2x √ó (-4) = -8x
Multiply the outer terms of the expression
Step 4: Calculate Inner
Inner: (-3) √ó x = -3x
Multiply the inner terms of the expression
Step 5: Calculate Last
Last: (-3) √ó (-4) = 12
Multiply the last terms in each bracket (negative √ó negative = positive)
Step 6: Combine all terms
2x2 + (-8x) + (-3x) + 12
Write all four products together
Step 7: Combine like terms
2x2 + (-8x - 3x) + 12 = 2x2 - 11x + 12
Combine the x terms: -8x - 3x = -11x
‚ú® Final Answer: 2x2 - 11x + 12 ‚ú®
Question 2:
(4y - x - 2)(5 - y)
Solution:
Step 1: Multiply the first bracket by the first term of the second bracket
5 √ó (4y - x - 2) = 20y - 5x - 10
Multiply each term in the first bracket by 5
Step 2: Multiply the first bracket by the second term of the second bracket
(-y) √ó (4y - x - 2) = -4y2 + xy + 2y
Multiply each term in the first bracket by -y
Step 3: Combine all terms
20y - 5x - 10 + (-4y2 + xy + 2y)
Write all products together
Step 4: Rearrange in standard form (highest powers first)
-4y2 + 20y + 2y + xy - 5x - 10
Group terms by their variable powers
Step 5: Combine like terms (y terms)
20y + 2y = 22y
Add the y terms together
Step 6: Write the final simplified expression
-4y2 + 22y + xy - 5x - 10
Combine all simplified terms in standard order
‚ú® Final Answer: -4y2 + 22y + xy - 5x - 10 ‚ú®
Question 3:
(2x - 3y)3
Solution:
Step 1: Write out the expansion
(2x - 3y)3 = (2x - 3y)(2x - 3y)(2x - 3y)
Express the cube as three factors multiplied together
Step 2: First multiply the first two brackets
(2x - 3y)(2x - 3y)
We'll use FOIL method for these two brackets
Step 3: Apply FOIL to the first two brackets
First: 2x √ó 2x = 4x2
Outer: 2x √ó (-3y) = -6xy
Inner: (-3y) √ó 2x = -6xy
Last: (-3y) √ó (-3y) = 9y2
Multiply each term using FOIL
Step 4: Combine like terms from first multiplication
4x2 + (-6xy - 6xy) + 9y2 = 4x2 - 12xy + 9y2
Combine the xy terms: -6xy - 6xy = -12xy
Step 5: Now multiply this result by the third bracket
(4x2 - 12xy + 9y2)(2x - 3y)
Multiply each term in the first bracket by each term in the second bracket
Step 6: Multiply by 2x
2x √ó (4x2 - 12xy + 9y2) = 8x3 - 24x2y + 18xy2
Distribute 2x to each term
Step 7: Multiply by -3y
(-3y) √ó (4x2 - 12xy + 9y2) = -12x2y + 36xy2 - 27y3
Distribute -3y to each term
Step 8: Combine all terms
8x3 - 24x2y + 18xy2 + (-12x2y + 36xy2 - 27y3)
Write all terms together
Step 9: Combine like terms (x¬≤y terms)
-24x2y - 12x2y = -36x2y
Add the x¬≤y terms
Step 10: Combine like terms (xy¬≤ terms)
18xy2 + 36xy2 = 54xy2
Add the xy¬≤ terms
Step 11: Write the final expanded form
8x3 - 36x2y + 54xy2 - 27y3
Arrange in descending order of powers
‚ú® Final Answer: 8x3 - 36x2y + 54xy2 - 27y3 ‚ú®
